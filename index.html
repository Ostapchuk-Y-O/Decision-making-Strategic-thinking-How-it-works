Competence Enhancement. How it works?

In this chapter, you will learn about what you need to do as part of your competency level enhancement training. Among all the competencies, of which Boosta identifies 11, you need to enhance one or more. This can be understood from the titles of the courses assigned to you. This chapter, a concise guide, will provide answers to all possible questions and help you understand what you need to do within the scope of your training.

Let's take a closer look at what you will be doing using the example of the competency group called 'Decision making'.

The Decision making competency group consists of four sub-competencies:

  • Strategic thinking
  • System thinking
  • Data driven
  • Open-mindedness
  • Flexibility
  • Decisions in conditions of uncertainty

Sub-competency can range from level - 1 to level 3.

Let's consider everything using the example of the sub-competency "Strategic thinking"...

Strategic thinking - devotes attention and time to think and form a way to achieve medium- and long-term goals.

Level -1 Doesn't form strategy. Often confuses the concept of strategy with the concept of goal.
Level 0 He has his own vision and strategy to achieve, but this strategy not always cooperates with the company's one. Thinks only in his area of responsibility.
Level 1 Pays attention to strategic issues and works on it. Clearly formulates a vision and metrics for the year that he wants to achieve as a result.
Level 2 Involves the team in the development of the strategy. Aligns business strategy with the business goals within team's direction.
Level 3 Indicates the direction of vision's development and the values through which it must be achieved.

ℹ️ This course is designed to enhance level 1.

The aim of this course is to teach you: 'Actively promotes ideas that can improve the performance of the company, brings them to the management.' This is the minimum required from you in your position and corresponds to your nearest growth area in this competency.

So, the allocated time for improving this sub-competency is 3 sections. During these three sections, your tasks will be as follows...

For example:

Below is an example. The tasks, their quantity, and order may vary across different sub-competencies.

👉 During the first section, your task will be:

Your task is to complete several assignments, including reading articles 📄, watching videos 🎥, and reading a book 📕. After each article and video, there will be three control questions ✏️✏️✏️, while no questions are provided after the book.

To move to the second section, you need to complete a "Case study section one".

A case study is an assignment where you are required to solve an educational case based on the material studied (videos, books, articles) and write your own response, which will be evaluated by the Instructor. Progress to the next section will only be unlocked after the assignment is accepted by the Instructor. If the assignment is rejected, you will need to revise it and submit it again.



👉 Second section:

The task for the second section is the same as for the first. Your assignment is to complete various tasks, such as reading articles 📄, watching videos 🎥, and reading a book 📕. After each article and video, there will be three control questions ✏️✏️✏️, while no questions are provided after the book.

To move to the third section, you need to complete a "Case study section two".

A case study is an assignment where you are required to solve an educational case based on the material studied (videos, books, articles) and write your own response, which will be evaluated by the Instructor. Progress to the next section will only be unlocked after the assignment is accepted by the Instructor. If the assignment is rejected, you will need to revise it and submit it again.



👉 Third section:

The tasks for the third section are the same as the previous two: videos 🎥, book 📕, and articles 📄. Note that the number of tasks may vary each section. For example, there may be only a book in the first or second section, while in the third section, there will be one article and two videos.

After studying all the provided materials in the third section, you will need to take the final test for the course, consisting of materials from all three sections. Successfully passing this test will signify the successful completion of the entire course.

👉 Your task is:
